CatFinder

CatFinder is a web app that allows people to pinpoint where they saw cats and upload cats pictures.


Project Statement

I’m a huge fan of cats. I want to see all the cats in my neighborhood. And wherever I go, I want to know if there are cute cats nearby me. Sometimes when I see a cat on the street, I wonder if I have seen that cat at somewhere else. So I created this web app to let people upload photos of random cats they saw on the street and share the location of where they saw the cats. The more people use this app, the more information the users know about cats around them!


Wireframe

Code

I used Firebase to store data on database, including cat photo, cat name, and location. And I used HTML, CSS, and JQuary to make the layout and functionality. The website is adaptive for various screen sizes.
